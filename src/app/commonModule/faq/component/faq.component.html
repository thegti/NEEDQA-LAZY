1. should i do any registrations to post my requirement ?

no registrations or payment is required to post a customer need.

2. is it a directory or a search engine?

no. unlike a directory or search engine; need.qa share your requirement with our registered merchants. they will be contacting you if they are capable of meeting your need.

3.how long it will take to get a response for my need?

merchants with the matching keywords are intimated as and when you post your need. they will get a download link which is active for the next 3 days. After that your requirement will not be visible to them.

4.Can i get product or services from outside Qatar?

Yes. Need.qa accepts supplier registrations from outside Qatar. Since the site is new to the market, it may take few more months to get response from Outside Qatar. Site admins are working on to get suppliers registered from Qatar and Outside Qatar so that you needs are met.

5.What to do if i am not getting any responses?

Responses are to be given by the merchants. Need.qa ensures the delivery of your requirement with the matched key words registered by the merchants. They are given a 3 days window to respond to your query. If you are not getting any queries in the stipulated 3 days, you can post your requirement one again and try.

6.What are Keywords?

Key words are those words which customers search for a product or service. Merchants are advised to register those keywords in their profile. Once its done, when a customer searches for a product or service with that keyword, those need will be diverted to the merchant. Key word includes a service name, a product name, a brand name or its combinations or alternatives.

7.How do i join as a merchant?

An individual or a business can join as a merchant. A person who handles sales job for the company will be the ideal beneficiary to join. To join, click on register as a merchant in the bottom panel. Initial few days will be trail period, further which you may choose a suitable plan to purchase.

8.Is there a registration and payment for Merchants?

Yes. As a supplier you need to register your credentials. There is a free trial period. After which you can choose a suitable paid plan.

9.How do i upload Key Words?

Once you register as a merchant, in merchant profile you have the option to individually upload your key words or upload it as a CSV file.

10.What do I do if i am not getting OTP / SMS?

OTP s are onetime passwords which are autogenerated. Normally it is delivered immediately on your given number. At times, if the Telecom provider has a queue it may get delayed. However, the OTP is shared to your email ID given. Please check the junk email also if it not in your in box.

11.What to do if i am not getting Email ?

Emails at times goes to your junk folder. Please check for the same and whitelist it or “mark as not junk” so that future mails are delivered to your in box.

12.I have not received my verification email for supplier registration ?

Once you register as a supplier, a verification email goes to the registered email. You may please check if the mail is delivered to your junk folder or quarantined by email policy of your organization. You may contact us on WhatsApp +974 66883800 for assistance.

13.Can a merchant advertise any product or service through this website?

No. This platform does not allow showcasing a product or service. It also does not publish any organization or brand name.

14.I am unable to download the enquiry as a merchant, why?

As a merchant, you are supposed to add key words to get the sales leads. Once you add, when a customer inputs the key word and post the details; if It matches the filters set by the merchant, it will get delivered as an email with a link and attachments if any. The link will be active for three days for you to respond. You may save the file separately for responding it later.

15.As a customer will I be getting unnecessary calls from merchants?

No. Your query is shared as a downloadable link and will expire in 3 days of you post. Hence, your details cannot be fetched after the timeline. If they have downloaded the file, they will contact you if the merchant can meet your requirement.

16.As a Merchant will I be getting all sales leads in my keywords list?

Merchants are capable of setting the sales leads based on the features mentioned in your profile. Merchants can decide on what type of queries are be to share with you.
